Sour Cream Enchiladas

Sour cream enchiladas are a mouthwatering Tex-Mex dish that combines the richness of sour cream with the bold flavors of traditional enchiladas. Soft tortillas are filled with a savory mixture, often consisting of seasoned shredded chicken or beef, before being generously smothered in a creamy sour cream sauce. Baked to golden perfection, these enchiladas deliver a satisfying blend of textures and tastes, with the tangy sour cream adding a delightful and comforting element to each bite.

Ingredients

  • Shredded cooked chicken or beef (pre-cooked and seasoned)
  • Onion, finely chopped
  • Garlic, minced
  • Cilantro, chopped
  • Salt and pepper
  • All-purpose flour
  • Chicken or vegetable broth
  • Sour cream
  • Green chilies, diced
  • Cumin
  • Chili powder

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven
  2. Prepare Filling
  3. Make Enchilada Sauce
  4. Assemble Enchiladas
  5. Pour Sauce Over Enchiladas
  6. Add Cheese
  7. Bake
  8. Optional Toppings
  9. Serve
