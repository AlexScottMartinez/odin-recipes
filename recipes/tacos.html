Mexican Street Tacos

Mexican Street Tacos are essential to Mexicans diet due to their deliciousness. It is a wonder how something so simple like meat within a tortilla can be so exquisite. To elevate the taste of the taco, one can add lemon/lime juice, chopped raw/fried onion, cilantro, and any kind of hot sauce.

Ingredients

  • Corn tortillas
  • Flank Steak
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Onions
  • Limes, plus extra for toppings
  • Cilantro
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Butter
  • Hot Sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Chop up the onion and cilantro
  3. Dip the tortilla in some vegetable oil and heat up both sides
  4. Put the meat and toppings on the tortilla
  5. Eat and enjoy
